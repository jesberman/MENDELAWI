MENDELAWI is a luxury evening wear brand. All of our designs are inspired by the natural beauty and elegance found within our fabrics. With the use of metallic accents paired with our sophisticated silhouettes, we strive to create pieces that exude the true radiance of all who wear them.

Brought to you by a mother-daughter duo, who’s shared passion for design has lead them to the foundation of MENDELAWI.

Shaina Kohn is the CEO and Design Lead of the brand. Growing up in the New York metro area she was exposed to the fashion industry at an early age. Shaina pursued her interest in fashion by attaining a degree in Fashion Design & Entrepreneurship. Her desire to experience couture fashion lead her to the start of her career in Paris, working for a textile company that partnered with couture houses. Upon her return to New York, Shaina expanded her love for high-end fashion working as a high jewelry designer. After several years, she became re-inspired by clothing and decided to start designing for others by forming MENDELAWI.

Cindy Mendelaw, Shaina’s mother, serves as the President and CFO of MENDELAWI. With a strong background in design and product development she has always had a creative drive herself. Cindy’s long career in sales has made her the business savvy key to all MENDELAWI operations. It is her mutual love and eye for fabrics that make her and Shaina the perfect team.
